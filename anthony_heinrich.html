Anthony Philip Heinrich(1781-1861)

Known as the "Beethoven of America". He was born in Bohemia in 1781 and emigrated to US in 1817. He is the first to write his large-scale orchestral works in America, by that time US orchestras were still developing.
He also focused on piano instrumental pieces like waltzes and march. His vocal work also shows his focus in the instrumental parts, often with brilliant piano accompaniment
