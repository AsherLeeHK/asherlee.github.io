Welcome to Victorian Music Archive!

Here you can find the MIDI file for tons of songs written from the period 1500 to 1927

About the author:

Asher Lee, a young guy from Hong Kong who loves the oldies written back at least 100 years ago.

Why love oldies music and when did you start your interest?

Why? Just personal taste maybe, I consider myself kind of strange, so I'm gonna love things others may not love LOL. I love them since 2015 and have arranged some old song since then. And by the end of 2022 I start to transcribe songs. (inspired by Benjamin Tubb and James Pitt-Payne)
