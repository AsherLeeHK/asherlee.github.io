Welcome to Victorian Music Archive!

Here you can find the MIDI file for tons of songs written from the period 1500 to 1927

About the author:

Asher Lee, a young guy from Hong Kong who loves the oldies written back at least 100 years ago.

Why love oldies music and when did you start your interest?

Why? Just personal taste maybe, I consider myself kind of strange, so I'm gonna love things others may find strange to love LOL. I love them since 2015 and have arranged some old songs since then. (Though musical arrangement is kind of painstaking, I don't produce much outcome)

And by the end of 2022 I started to transcribe scores for old songs, this brings me more outcome (also amusement, as I can enjoy and appreciate the creativity harnessed by the bygone musicians) than the past year of arranging songs. My work is inspired by Benjamin Tubb and Sheet Music Singer. Their output is enormous, notably Dr. James Pitt-Payne (who works with SMS) who's sequenced 14000 MIDIs throughout 20 years!
